In-Depth Case Studies

Brand Launch Reel

Client problem: Raw footage lacked energy for social ads.

Creative approach: Hook-first edit, animated captions, product closeups.

Process: Selects, music sync, motion graphics, color grade.

Outcome: Increased Instagram reel engagement by 45% through dynamic editing and animated captions.

YouTube Series Edit

Client problem: Long episodes had weak viewer retention.

Creative approach: Strong intro, chapter flow, pattern breaks.

Motion graphics: Lower thirds, callouts, chapter cards.

Outcome: Better pacing and clearer viewer journey.

Before vs After Visual Refresh

Before: Flat color, noisy audio, basic captions.

After: Color grade, sound cleanup, animated captions, clean export.

Metrics: Higher watch time and stronger brand perception.
