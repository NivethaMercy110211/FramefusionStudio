Quote clarity

What affects your final quote.

Every project is different. These four factors determine the final price — understanding them helps you brief accurately and receive a realistic estimate on the first reply.

🎬
Raw Footage Length

Longer recordings need more review time, stronger organization, and clearer story selection before editing begins.

✨
Animation Depth

Simple captions, custom lower thirds, UI animations, and advanced After Effects motion each affect scope and delivery time.

📦
Platform Versions

One master edit can become reels, shorts, ad ratios, thumbnails, and clean archive versions — each adds preparation time.

🔍
Revision Structure

Clear timestamps, one feedback source, and grouped notes keep revision rounds fast and pricing predictable for both sides.

Simple process

How billing works.

From first brief to final invoice — a transparent, four-step process with no surprises, no hidden fees, and clear communication at every stage.

01

Submit Your Brief

Share your footage, project goals, target platform, deadline, and any style references using our contact form or direct message. The more detail you provide, the faster we can scope your project accurately.

  • Platform & format requirements
  • Raw footage length estimate
  • Visual style references
02

Receive Your Quote

Within 24 hours you'll receive a fully itemized quote outlining the recommended plan tier, any add-ons, delivery timeline, and revision structure. No vague estimates — every line is explained.

  • Itemized pricing breakdown
  • Delivery timeline confirmed
  • Revision rounds outlined
03

Approve & Kickoff

Once you approve the quote, a 50% deposit secures your production slot. Editing begins immediately — you'll receive a project kickoff summary with milestones, check-in points, and your first delivery estimate.

  • 50% deposit to start
  • Production slot reserved
  • Milestone dates confirmed
04

Deliver & Final Pay

After revisions are approved and the final master file is ready, the remaining 50% balance is billed before full file handoff. You'll receive all platform-optimized exports, source files, and a delivery summary.

  • Final balance on approval
  • All exports & source files
  • 30-day post-delivery support
